Salarpuria Sattva Group

Salarpuria Sattva Group

This Group emerged in the background of Salarpuria and diversified its operations based in Bangalore. It is well known for its Tech Parks, Office Space, Premium Residential buildings and Upmarket Commercial Complexes.
The parent organisation, Salarpuria Group was established in 1985 by Late Shri G. D. Salarpuria in Kolkata. The group is now headed by the young and dynamic Bijay Agarwal whose vision and passion has turned it into a truly multi-diversified Group which not only prides in meeting clients satisfaction but also communities where it operates.

Creating world class residential and commercial spaces:

Given its motto of being in the right place at the right time and delivering excellence, Salarpuria Sattva group has proved to be an innovator and creator of structures that are truly world class in both quality and style in the real estate segment. The Group has offered world class commercial IT spaces, business complexes and housing projects to its customers.
Multi-diversified Group
The group which is already into Real Estate Development, Special Economic Zone Development, Infrastructure, Township Development, Facility Management business, has forayed into the IT segment with Sattva e-Tech and also into education with the take over of the management of Greenwood High International School. The Group is also planning to enter into the logistics division shortly to diversify its operations further.
Within the real estate industry too, Salarpuria Sattva group has diversified by expanding its property management division to cover the management of not just in-house projects but also to provide management services for other projects of real estate firms.
Over the last two and half decades, the Group has had the distinction of constructing close to 13 million sq ft in its 85 odd projects spanning both residential and commercial. Its first project after entering the Bangalore market in the early nineties was Money Chambers, in year 1993.

Presence in other cities:
Besides Bangalore and Kolkata, Salarpuria Sattva group has ongoing and completed projects in other cities too like Hyderabad, Pune, Vishakhapatnam and Jaipur.
