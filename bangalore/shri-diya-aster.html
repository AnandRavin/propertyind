Specifications

STRUCTURE:
Seismic ll compliant RCC framed structure using ready mix concrete.

WALLS:
6" concrete block masonry for external walls
4" concrete block masonry for internal walls.

DOORS:
Main DOOR;  
Teak wood frame with HDF shutters - prefab engineered panel door, lock handle, brass hinges, tower bolt, stopper & magic eye.

OTHER DOORS:
Sal wood frame & flush door shutters with powder coated fittings.

WINDOWS:
Aluminum windows with glass and MS grill protection.

FLOORING:
Quality vitrified tiles - Drawing room, dining, bedrooms & kitchen, anti - skid ceramic tiles for Toilet and Balcony.

KITCHEN:
20mm thick polished granite counter top platform with stainless steel Sink & 2’-0”ht ceramic  dadoing above the granite counter top. Provision for Aqua Guard point washing machine in utility area.

LIFTS:
6 passenger capacity lift of reputed make with generator back-up, in each block.

TOILETS:
Ceramic glazed tiles, dado up to 7' - 0"ht, Matching 50% colour. EWC with flush tank of ISI make. One hot & cold mixture unit for shower & all other fittings of ISI make in toilets. Provision for geyser & exhaust  fan.

LOBBIES & STAIRCASE:
All lobbies & staircases elegantly finished in Granite & tiles.

PAINTING:
OBD paint for internal walls - roller finish. Cement paint for external walls over plastered surface.

ELECTRICAL:
One TV/Telephone point in the living and master bed room. Provision for 1 split AC in master bedroom. Elegant modular electrical switch of ISI make. Quality copper cables.

BACKUP POWER:
100% backup power available for all common area, lifts, lobbies & staircases. 0.5 KVA back power for each apartment for lights & fan points.

WATER SUPPLY:
Bore well water, pump, sump, overhead tank provided with concealed pipeline.

INTERCOM:
One extension to each flat.
