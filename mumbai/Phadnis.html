Eastern Pune’s Lifestyle Township- “Eastern Ranges” 

Phadnis Properties, part of the fast growing and prestigious Phadnis Group, has launched its ambitious new township-‘Eastern Ranges’ in Keshavanagar, Mundhawa,Pune. This new township is being viewed as the residence of an upwardly mobile young Pune of tomorrow. This 18 acre township has been planned to make the most of natural day light and fresh air. Variety of choices it has something to suit everybody’s taste, requirement and budget.  

This modern township is conveniently located and offers multiple amenities making living comfortable. The range of these amenities includes well equipped hospitals, schools and shopping malls. The entertainment and leisure destinations like multiplexes, 5 star hotels are all within a distance of three to eight kilometers that is a maximum travel time of twenty to twenty five minutes from this futuristic township. 

Phadnis Group has gone to great lengths to see that their mini township is filled with top of the line amenities like a two level elegant entrance lobby, invertors for each apartment, putting green, a club house,   children’s play area, library, senior citizen sit-outs, private parking space sufficient for two cars per apartment, ample visitors parking, solar energy water heating and even rain water harvesting to name a few. In addition to these minute facilities, the security structure has been given key importance with an installation of CCTV and ultra modern surveillance system.
