Alibaug – An album of blissful images –wet footprints on sunlit beaches that get washed by moonlit waves...A home in such a place is no longer a distant dream... In this fast growing city of fishing hamlets, red soil and enchanting beaches, lies the “Nautilus” A bouquet of dream homes in the laps of this calm city.

Alibaug is situated in the womb of Konkan beltand is one of the best places for a holiday home with an immense investment potential and it is here that MARK ventures have come up with “Nautilus”, an “integrated township” of studio homes, 1 BHK and 2 BHK flats.


Nautilus is an artistically designed integrated township which has camouflaged itself in the theme of Konkan. It is connected to Pune via road and to Mumbai via road as well as ferries. True to its name, Nautilus is a world; complete in its own way fulfilled with premium amenities for an urban life...and yet studded amidst the nature...You will notice a lift in your mood when you see a single glimpse of this tempting avenue. And a grand gateway graciously welcomes and escorts you into the mesmerizing ambience of Nautilus. The feeling of aesthetics and strength is expressed through every part of the architecture – the arches, the windows, the balconies and also the elegant sloping roofs.

The club house eager to host your special occasions which will convert into pleasant memories. Enjoy and relax yourself in the spiral shaped swimming pool. Overlooking this splash’n‘plunge, is the party lawn ready to be a part of the most prized events of your life. Your wish to be treated like royalty, the Jacuzzi is forever there awaiting your command.      The amphitheatre is a great open space for creative interaction. Your little ones can shower nuggets of pleasure in the lap of absolute safety of this play area. The elegant sit outs under the canopy of fragrant flowers shall provide the perfect setting for the senior citizens for a light hearted tête-à-tête in the evenings. With most modern equipped gymnasium to make you fit. And Lots more to explore….

Enjoy the blissful living in Nautilus... and re- enjoy the missed moments in your life
