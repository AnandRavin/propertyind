About Project
AMRAI NAGRI located at Karjat known for its beautiful, scenic and hilly hamlet full of Mango Trees well connected by rail and road comes close to a fantasy. Your stay at AMRAI NAGRI offers rejuvenation, recreation and relaxation. Lying nestled between the majestic mountains of Matheran, Murbad and Lonavala, the peace and natural beauty of the place soothe and heal all those experience it. Tired of the never ending stressful life of the city? Looking out for a way to soothe those nerves? Then the answer lies in one name and that’s AMRAI NAGRI. It is a promise of peace for all who want to have a great deal of it in the future. Come and relax and let nature take its own course to rejuvenate you. 

Situated in the lap of greenery and enclosed by scenic beauty that echoes the divinity, AMRAI NAGRI is located in the beautiful town of Karjat. Out here every soul gets an opportunity to revel in the bounties of Mother Nature and feel the way you have never felt before. 

Besides having imprints of the glorious Maratha Empire and a short distance from popular hill stations like Matheran, Lonavla and the famous Bhimashankar Temple, Karjat is also going to be the new haven for Hollywood Studio giants like 20th Century Fox and Walt Disney and already has the well established ND Studios. From the ecstatic moments of getting caressed by the gentle drops of rain to eavesdropping to the melodious twitter of every crooning bird AMRAI NAGRI humbly presents to each one of you sites of immense personal satisfaction all within the beauty and tranquility that nature has to offer. 

Developed by Vijaylaxmi Construction renowned for providing locations for additional houses popularly known as Second Homes in and around mumbai, AMRAI NAGRI is one project which is waiting for those seeking everlasting serenity and harmony.
