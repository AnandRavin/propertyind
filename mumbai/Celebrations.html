 Come home to nature at Celebrations County. Located at Talegaon, 5 mins ride from Mumbai-Pune bypass, it is located admist lush green lawns over a hill slope overlooking a river. With a picturesque location this site is no less than a serene hill station.

Luxurious 1 & 2 BHK apartments on the hill top. The only 11 storied residential project in Talegaon, the tallest and highest building in Talegaon. The luxury apartment scheme includes facilities such as a clubhouse, sparkling water bodies and exotic river side views etc.
