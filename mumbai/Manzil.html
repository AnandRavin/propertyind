The touch of Pragmatism

Dronagiri is beautiful place surrounded by Hills, Creeks and Sea from three sides. It's a place where you would find natural habitant singing and composing a rhythmic tune just for you,every morning.

Dronagiri is one of the 14 nodal townships of Navi Mumbai, connected by National Highway (NH) 4B, State Highway(SH) 41 & the Panvel- Urban existing Railway Line, which is on it way of conversion for commuter traffic. It is proposed to have hovercraft connectivity with Greater Mumbai.

Dronagiri is located in the near vicinity of Jawaharlal Nehru Port, have Private & Public sector offices, complexes, warehousing facilities and an exclusive residential area.
