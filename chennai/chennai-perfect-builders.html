About Perfect Builders

Perfect Builders and Interiors, is an young and fastly growing construction company in chennai, Madipakkam. Perfect builders primarily concentrating in quality flat promotions in chennai.

Perfect Builders was found by Late.Mr.C.Somasundara Pandian on 2005 who was a pioneer in Construction and Hardware Material Industry under the name of Thiruchendur Hardware in Madipakkam since 1960.
