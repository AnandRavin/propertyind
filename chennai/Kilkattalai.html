Keelakattalai, Chennai

Keelakattalai is a suburb in chennai. The Velachery MRTS station is nearer than the St. Thomas Mount station, about 5km in North. Keelakattalai is situated 6kms away from the chennai international airport. The St. Thomas Mount station covers the distance from Marina beach to Chengalpattu. The Velachery station connects to the places like Adyar, Tiruvanmiyur etc. The inner ring road project work is complete and connects to GST road and then towards airport or Ashok nagar.
